How It Works
  • Tap the image area or the photo icon to select a photo from your library.
  • Choose the language you want the text translated into.
  • Optionally select an aspect ratio to crop the image.
  • Tap Translate and wait a few seconds.
  • Your translated image appears with the text replaced in place.
  • Save the result to your Photos library using the download icon.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why is my translation taking a long time?

Translation requires sending your image to our server for AI processing. This typically takes 5–15 seconds depending on image complexity and your internet connection. Please ensure you have a stable connection.

The translation result doesn't look accurate. What can I do?

AI translations work best on clear, well-lit images with readable text. Try using a higher quality photo, ensuring the text is not blurry or obscured, and cropping tightly around the text area before translating.

Can I translate text from screenshots?

Yes. Any image in your photo library — including screenshots — can be used. Simply select it from your library and proceed normally.

How do I restore my subscription after reinstalling?

Open the app, tap the crown icon to open the upgrade screen, then tap Restore Purchases at the bottom. Make sure you are signed in to the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through Apple. Go to Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions on your iPhone or iPad, find Image Translate, and tap Cancel Subscription.

Is my data private? Are my photos stored?

Your photos are transmitted securely to our server only to perform the translation and are deleted immediately afterwards. We never store your images. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

How do I delete my translation history?

Your translation history is stored only on your device. You can clear it from within the History tab in the app. Deleting the app also removes all local data.

Which languages are supported?

Image Translate currently supports 22 languages: English, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Japanese, Chinese (Simplified & Traditional), Korean, Arabic, Russian, Hindi, Dutch, Polish, Turkish, Vietnamese, Thai, Swedish, Indonesian, Ukrainian, and Malay.
